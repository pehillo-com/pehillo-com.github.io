Things To Do and See

When you arrive at Pehillo, we’ll help you organize your trip based on the types of activities you’re interested in. From visits to local markets and villages, to hiking in the countryside and finding the best restaurants in the region - we'll help you organize your trip from start to finish.

Here’s what you can expect from your time in the Lot-et-Garrone:

Visit Nerac & surrounding villages and cities

About a 5 minute drive from Pehillo, you’ll find the charming historic city of Nerac where you can easily spend a few days exploring local markets, boating along the Baise river, visiting Henry IV’s castle and museum, trying local cuisine and simply strolling up and down the city’s beautiful narrow streets.

Pehillo is also located close to many beautiful cities and villages ideal for day trips including Bordeaux, Toulouse, Condon, Saint-Emilion and many more. All of these cities will allow you to explore different parts of the culture and traditions of the Southwest of France.

Sporting Activities

Want to stay active during your vacation? In Nerac and the surrounding area, you’ll find opportunities to bike, zip line, play tennis and horseback ride.

Water Parks

The region’s largest water parks is about a 30 minute drive from Pehillo - from wave pools to water slides and everything in between, there’s no doubt you’ll find fun for the whole family.

Spas & Thermal Baths

The region is known for its thermal baths, a tradition in the Lot-et-Garonne since the Roman times. Many of them offer full spa services that makes for a perfect day trip to relax and unwind during your vacation.
